CFB IT Internet Connectivity


Your internet connectivity is the heart of your business. A slow or unreliable internet connection makes efficiency impossible. CFBIT can help you find faster and more reliable internet provision, for less than you’re spending today.

Fiber (Shared or Private)

Fiber is the most reliable, speedy, and scalable access mode with speeds up from 20 Meg to 10 Gig. We can find out if this is an option in your location and help you obtain the best available rates.


Ethernet over Copper

EoC technology provides dedicated and synchronous speeds of up to 100 MBPS at lower prices than you would expect with traditional T1 and NxT1 services.


Cable Modem (High Speed)

Cable broadband provides asynchronous and inexpensive bandwidth internet, which can be a great option for redundant connections or for smaller companies than do not require a dedicated service.


Fixed Wireless

Fixed wireless internet offers dedicated and synchronous speeds up to 1 Gig for general use and private data transport. It’s a great option for business needing diversity or a rapid installation of service.


Cellular

Cellular internet access is a low-cost option for temporary or backup service.
